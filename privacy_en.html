StudyTime Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 17, 2025

Effective Date: December 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to StudyTime (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). This Privacy Policy is intended to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. By using the App, you agree to the data processing practices described in this Privacy Policy.

The App is developed and operated by RikSong. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the contact information provided at the end of this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register an account, we collect your email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Information: You may optionally provide your avatar, nickname, and personal bio.
  • Study Goal Data: Your study goals, plans, and related information.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Study Records: Including study duration, start/end times, and timer modes used (free mode/Pomodoro mode).
  • Statistical Data: Study duration statistics, consecutive check-in days, study circle rankings, etc.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, timezone settings, language preferences.
  • App Usage Data: Features and frequency of your app usage.

2.3 Subscription and Payment Information

  • Subscription Status: Your Pro membership subscription status, subscription type (monthly/yearly), and expiration date.
  • Note: Actual payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not store your credit card numbers or other payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing Core Services

  • Recording and syncing your study data
  • Generating study statistics reports
  • Providing study circle features and leaderboard services
  • Managing your Pro membership subscription

3.2 Improving Services

  • Analyzing app usage patterns to improve user experience
  • Identifying and fixing technical issues
  • Developing new features

3.3 Communication

  • Sending important service notifications
  • Responding to your inquiries and support requests

4. Information Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Some study data is stored locally on your device to support offline use.
  • Cloud Storage: To enable data synchronization and study circle features, we use Supabase cloud services to store your data. Servers are located in AWS ap-south-1 (Mumbai, India).

4.2 Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Using SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted password storage
  • Implementing access control and authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits

4.3 Data Retention

  • We retain your data while you use the App.
  • If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days.
  • Certain anonymized statistical data may be retained for service improvement.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

5.1 Study Circle Features

  • When you join a study circle, other members can see your username, avatar, and study duration statistics.
  • The leaderboard displays your ranking and study data.

5.2 Third-Party Service Providers

  • Supabase: For data storage and user authentication
  • Apple: For processing Pro membership subscriptions and payments

5.3 Legal Requirements

When required by laws and regulations or in response to legal processes

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

6.1 Right to Access

You can view your profile and study data within the App.

6.2 Right to Rectification

You can modify your information on the profile page.

6.3 Right to Erasure

You can delete your account and data by:

  • Going to "Settings" > "Account" > "Delete Account" in the App
  • Contacting us via email to request deletion

6.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request to export your study data. Please contact us via email.

6.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can stop using the App or delete your account at any time.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13, we will immediately delete such information.

If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Auto-Renewal Subscriptions

8.1 Subscription Terms

  • Pro membership is managed through Apple App Store subscriptions
  • Two subscription plans are available: monthly and yearly
  • Subscription fees are charged to your Apple ID account upon confirmation of purchase

8.2 Auto-Renewal

  • Subscriptions will automatically renew unless you turn off auto-renewal at least 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period
  • Renewal fees will be charged within 24 hours before the end of the current subscription period
  • After purchase, you can manage and cancel subscriptions in your App Store account settings

8.3 Canceling Subscriptions

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open iPhone "Settings"
  2. Tap your name > "Subscriptions"
  3. Select StudyTime Pro
  4. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

9. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties and recommend that you review their respective privacy policies.

10. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

For significant changes, we will notify you through in-app notifications or other means. Continued use of the App indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
