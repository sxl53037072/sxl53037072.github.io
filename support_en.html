‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start using the Pomodoro Timer?

A: Open the app and tap "Start Study" on the home screen. Select Pomodoro mode, set your focus duration, and begin timing. Your study session will be automatically recorded upon completion.

Q: Will I lose my study data?

A: After signing in, your study data is automatically synced to the cloud. You won't lose any data even if you switch devices. Note that guest mode only saves data locally.

Q: How do I join a Study Circle?

A: Go to the "Study Circles" page where you can join a friend's circle using an invite code, or create your own circle and invite friends to join.

Q: What are Pro membership benefits?

A: Pro members enjoy unlimited statistics access, more Study Circle slots, exclusive avatar frames, ad-free experience, and other premium features.

Q: How do I cancel my subscription?

A: Open iPhone "Settings" > Tap your Apple ID > "Subscriptions" > Find "FocusCroc" and cancel. After cancellation, you can still use Pro features until the current billing period ends.

Q: How do I request a refund?

A: Refunds are processed through Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the purchase, and submit a refund request.
